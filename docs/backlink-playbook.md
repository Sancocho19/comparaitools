# Backlink playbook for ComparAITools

## Principle
Do not chase random low-quality links. Build pages worth citing.

## Assets worth promoting
1. AI pricing index
2. best AI tools by role report
3. quarterly changes in AI tool pricing
4. tool comparison benchmarks
5. “what changed this month” summaries with clear source trail

## Outreach buckets
- AI newsletters
- indie hacker / startup blogs
- SaaS review blogs
- niche YouTube creators with websites
- university / learning resource pages when the page genuinely helps students

## Pitch angles
- fresh data on tool pricing changes
- side-by-side tool comparisons with current facts
- role-based buying guides
- a benchmark or index page updated monthly

## Link safety rules
- no mass spam
- no fake guest posts on junk sites
- no private blog network garbage
- no paid links unless clearly disclosed and compliant

## Internal linking rules
Every money page should link to:
- the core tool page
- at least 2 related compare pages
- at least 1 category page
- at least 2 blog pages with adjacent intent
